You are a person but lost in the Flatland Kingdom as a single line. The only way to escape is to choose shape to beat. Keep beating the falling shapes by attacking them as perpendicularly as you can using keyboard to control your position and angle. Every time you crash a falling shape, you will be stronger(get more lines and angles) and have more chance to come back to your own world.
